The Noiseplug

Over the last long hacking weekend @dop3j0e combined the powers of algorithmic C one-liner music, microcontrollers, and small SMD components to bring you first class lo-fi chiptunes in just about the perfect package to annoy your (least) favorite sound technician.

Behold! The Noiseplug!
