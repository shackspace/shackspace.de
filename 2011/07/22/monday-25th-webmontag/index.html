Webmontag

shackspace will be hosting Webmontag on Monday July 25th.
The event will start at 19:00 with an informal get-together.
Starting 19:30 there will be talks and presentations.

Topics covered are, besides others, Ambiguität im Interface-Design by Dragan Espenschied and Open Source Ecology by Nikolay Georgiev.

More about this month’s Webmontag can be found on the wiki page for the event.
To learn more about Webmontag in general, check out the Webmontag website.

Feel free to drop by and check it out!
