Made at shackspace: shacklogger

Getting your big and small projects noticed is now easier than ever before.
We’ve had a shackspace tumblr account for a while now (you might have noticed the widget to the right of this article) but accessing it wasn’t that easy workflow-wise.

Now, thanks to resident hacker makefu - who sponsored his old Nexus 4 phone - there’s shacklogger. You can simply snap a photo or record a brief video of your project, write a few short lines of description and directly upload it to the log.shackspace.de tumblr account for the world to see!
